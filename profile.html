Rahul Vaniya

Director | Writer | Producer

The visionary director behind Orange Frame Production

About

Rahul Vaniya, the visionary director, writer, and producer at the helm of Orange Frame Production, embodies the relentless pursuit of cinematic excellence. With a keen eye for the nuances of human emotion and societal undercurrents, Rahul has carved a niche in the Indian entertainment landscape through his compelling narratives and innovative storytelling techniques.

His directorial debut, the web series "Anti 2020" streaming on Hungama & MX Player, garnered widespread acclaim for its bold exploration of contemporary issues amidst the global pandemic.

Rahul's passion for storytelling transcends mere entertainment; it is a profound dedication to illuminating the human condition. Drawing inspiration from diverse cultural tapestries and personal experiences, he strives to create films that resonate deeply, provoke thought, and inspire change.
