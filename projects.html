Our Projects

Anti 2020

TV Mini Series (2022) - Director

In the shadow of a global crisis, "Anti 2020" delves into the lives of ordinary individuals grappling with isolation, loss, and unexpected connections. This gripping series explores the resilience of the human spirit amidst uncertainty, blending dark humor with poignant reflections on society and survival.

Vrukshree

Short (2024) - Director

"Vrukshree" is a poetic short film that celebrates the enduring strength of nature and the quiet heroism of rural life. Through vivid imagery and subtle storytelling, it portrays a young woman's journey of self-discovery, intertwined with the rhythms of the earth and the whispers of ancestral wisdom.

Shuruaat Ka Twist

(2019) - Director

This experimental narrative twists the conventions of beginnings, exploring how a single unexpected event can unravel the fabric of destiny. "Shuruaat Ka Twist" is a meditation on chance, choice, and the intricate dance between fate and free will, presented through a lens of surreal elegance.

Getaway: The Short Film

Short (2023) - Producer

"Getaway" is an adrenaline-fueled escape into the unknown, where two strangers embark on a road trip that becomes a metaphor for liberation and self-reckoning. This short film captures the thrill of breaking free from constraints, set against breathtaking landscapes and underscored by a haunting score.
